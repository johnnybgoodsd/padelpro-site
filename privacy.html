Privacy Policy

Padel Pro · Last updated 3 July 2026

Padel Pro (“the app,” “we,” “us”) is built to respect your privacy. This policy explains what we collect — and the short version is: nothing.

1. Information we collect

None. Padel Pro does not collect, transmit, or sell any personal information. There are no user accounts, no advertising, and no third-party analytics or tracking SDKs. The app makes no network calls to us.

2. Data you create in the app

Players, scores, tournaments, ratings, and leagues you enter are stored locally on your device. If you turn on iCloud sync, that data is synced privately through your own Apple iCloud account under your control — we never receive or have access to it.

3. Notifications

If you grant notification permission, the app schedules a single local reminder before a free trial ends, so you are never charged by surprise. This notification is generated on your device; nothing is sent to us or any server.

4. Subscriptions and payments

Subscriptions are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We never see or receive your payment details. Apple’s Privacy Policy governs that transaction.

5. Sharing and disclosure

We do not share, sell, rent, or disclose any data — because we do not collect any. Result cards you choose to share are sent only through the destinations you pick (Messages, etc.) using Apple’s share sheet.

6. Children’s privacy

Padel Pro is not directed at children under 13 and collects no personal information from anyone, including children.

7. Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new date.
